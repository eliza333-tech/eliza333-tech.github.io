01 — Logo

Logo System

The GoDark mark combines a bold geometric G-form with a dynamic inner cursor symbol, representing speed and private execution.

Do

  • Use the icon on white, dark, or black backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space of at least 1× the icon height on all sides
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or distort
  • Use the wordmark "GoDark" alongside the icon at smaller sizes
  • Place on solid or very subtle gradient backgrounds only

Don't

  • Recolor or alter the gradient in the inner mark
  • Apply drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
  • Place on busy, photographic, or patterned backgrounds
  • Rotate, skew, or add decorative borders
  • Use the icon smaller than 24px in any context

06 — Voice & Tone

Voice & Tone

GoDark speaks with precision and confidence — never flashy, never verbose. We are the serious tool for serious traders.

Direct
No fluff. Lead with the benefit. Every sentence earns its place. Traders are busy — respect their time.
Confident
We don't "try to" do things — we do them. Assertive language without arrogance. The tech speaks for itself.
Trustworthy
Privacy is serious business. Measured, factual copy. Never hype. Claims are specific and backed by the protocol.
Say
"Trade any token. Zero slippage. No exposure."
Don't Say
"Experience the revolutionary next-gen DeFi trading platform of the future!"
Say
"Your orders are hidden from the mempool until execution."
Don't Say
"We try our best to keep your trades as private as possible."
